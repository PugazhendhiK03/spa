Terms & Conditions

Booking Policy

All appointments require a credit card to hold the reservation. We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.

Cancellation Policy

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling of appointments. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance will result in a charge of 50% of the reserved service amount. No-shows will be charged 100% of the reserved service amount.

Late Arrivals

If you arrive late for your appointment, your treatment time may be shortened to accommodate other guests. Full payment for the scheduled service will still apply.

Health Conditions

Please advise us of any health conditions, allergies, injuries, pregnancy, or special needs when making your appointment. Some services may not be appropriate for certain conditions.

Gift Certificates

Gift certificates are non-refundable and not redeemable for cash. Lost or stolen gift certificates cannot be replaced. Gift certificates do not expire.

Minimum Age

Guests under 18 years of age must have parental consent to receive services. Some services may have additional age restrictions.

Gratuity

Gratuities are not included in service prices and are left to the discretion of our guests. The standard gratuity for spa services is 15-20%.

Cancellation Policy

Appointment Cancellation

Your spa treatments are reserved especially for you. We value your business and ask that you respect the spa's scheduling policies. Should you need to cancel or reschedule, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance.

Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance will result in a charge equal to 50% of the reserved service amount.

"No shows" will be charged 100% of the reserved service amount.

Late Arrival

All appointments have been designed to allow appropriate time for full enjoyment of the service. Your late arrival may limit our ability to offer the fullest possible experience. Please be aware that late arrivals will not be afforded extension of scheduled treatments. Treatments will be rendered only for the remainder of the scheduled appointment and you will be responsible for payment of the full service.

Group Bookings

For parties of three or more, please call to book your group appointment and a 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. Group cancellation policy requires 7 days notice of cancellation or the deposit is forfeited.

Special Considerations

Please notify the spa coordinator of any allergies or sensitivities to aromas or ingredients when you schedule your appointment. Although we offer planned spa packages for your convenience, you are welcome to combine any of our spa and salon services for a custom package that meets your needs.
