About Us

Our story, mission, and team

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Luxe Bali Spa began with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where men could experience premium spa treatments in an environment designed specifically for them.

Our founder, Arjun Kapoor, recognized the need for a space where men could comfortably enjoy the benefits of spa therapies without the typical spa stereotypes.

What started as a single treatment room has grown into Mumbai's premier men's spa, known for our expertise in male-specific treatments and our commitment to excellence.

Our Mission & Values

Client-Centric Care

Every treatment we offer is tailored to suit the preferences and needs of our clients. Your comfort is our top priority.

Confidentiality

We understand that spa visits can be personal. We provide a discreet, respectful space where you can relax with confidence.

Authenticity

We stay true to traditional techniques while blending them with modern wellness trends to offer the best of both worlds.
