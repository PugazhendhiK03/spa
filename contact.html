Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my appointment?

We provide all necessary amenities including robes, slippers, and towels. You may want to bring comfortable clothing to change into after your treatments. For massages, you'll be properly draped at all times, so undergarments are optional based on your comfort level.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

We recommend arriving 15-20 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. This allows you to complete any necessary paperwork, change into a robe, and relax in our lounge before your treatment begins.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service price. No-shows will be charged the full price of the scheduled service.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Gift certificates are available for any amount or specific services. They can be purchased online or at our spa and never expire. Gift certificates make perfect gifts for birthdays, anniversaries, holidays, or just to show someone you care.

Are gratuities included?

Gratuities are not included in our service prices but are greatly appreciated by our staff. A standard gratuity of 15-20% is customary for spa services, similar to restaurant tipping. You may add gratuity when booking online or provide it directly to your therapist.
