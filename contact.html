Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my treatment?

For massage treatments, we recommend comfortable clothing. During the treatment, you will be properly draped for privacy. For facials, you can remain fully clothed.

Do I need to book in advance?

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to ensure your preferred time and therapist are available. However, walk-ins are welcome based on availability.

Can I choose a male therapist?

Yes, all our therapists are highly trained male professionals specializing in treatments for men. You may request a specific therapist when booking.

What if I'm running late?

If you arrive late, your session may be shortened to avoid delays for the next client. Full service charges will still apply.

Are there any contraindications for massage?

Yes, if you have medical conditions such as infections, fever, open wounds, or recent surgeries, please inform us before your session. A doctor’s note may be required in some cases.

Is tipping expected?

Tipping is appreciated but not mandatory. If you’re happy with your service, a 10-15% tip is customary.
